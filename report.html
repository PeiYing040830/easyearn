FAQ

Reporting FAQ

Quick answers about safety, verification, and response timelines.

How long does it take to review a report?

Most reports are reviewed within 24–48 hours. High-risk cases may be addressed sooner.

Will the employer know I reported them?

No. Reports are confidential and reviewed only by admins.

What evidence should I upload?

Provide screenshots, messages, job links, or any proof that helps verify the issue.

Can I report even if I am not registered?

Yes. You can submit a report with your email and details of the incident.
