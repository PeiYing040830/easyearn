Overview

Platform Rules

By using EasyEarn, users agree to follow the platform guidelines and act responsibly.

Provide accurate information during registration.
Employers must clearly state job details and payment terms.
Job seekers must follow agreed schedules and job requirements.
Reports of fraud or misconduct will be investigated by Admin.
Responsibilities

What We Expect

Clear expectations help ensure a trusted and safe gig economy environment.

Job Seekers

  • Arrive on time and communicate delays
  • Complete agreed tasks and schedules
  • Maintain respectful communication

Employers

  • Provide clear job scopes and pay
  • Respect worker rights and safety
  • Avoid misleading or false listings

Platform Use

  • Do not share false or harmful content
  • Respect data privacy and access rules
  • Report abuse or suspicious activity

Terms FAQ

Quick answers about behavior, reporting, and account safety.

What happens if a user violates the rules?

Accounts may be reviewed, warnings issued, or access restricted based on severity.

Employers must follow fair practices. Reports are reviewed by admins when necessary.

How do I report misconduct?

Use the Report page to provide details. Admins investigate and take action.

Terms may be updated. Users will be notified of major changes.
