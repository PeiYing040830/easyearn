FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick guidance for job seekers and employers.

How do I create a Job Seeker account?

Go to Register, select Job Seeker, and complete your profile with skills and location.

How do employers get verified?

Employers can request verification after registration. Verified badges are shown on profiles.

Can I track my application status?

Yes. Each application has a timeline showing Pending, Reviewed, Accepted, and Rejected.

What if I see a suspicious job posting?

Use the Report page to flag suspicious listings or employers for admin review.

Is my data protected?

We follow PDPA 2010 with role-based access control, HTTPS, and Supabase Row Level Security policies.

How can I generate a resume?

The Work History Dashboard auto-generates a PDF resume from completed jobs.
