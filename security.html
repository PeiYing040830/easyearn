FAQ

Security FAQ

Quick answers about protection and privacy on EasyEarn.

How is my data protected?

We use HTTPS, Supabase authentication, and RLS policies to protect personal data and prevent unauthorized access.

How do you handle reports?

Reports are reviewed by admins. High-risk cases are prioritized for immediate action.

Can employers access job seeker data?

Only data required for matching is visible. Sensitive data is restricted by role-based rules.

What if I find a suspicious listing?

Use the Report page to submit details for admin review and investigation.
