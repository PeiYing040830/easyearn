What makes a strong listing

A clear, specific post helps the right job seekers match with you faster.

Listing quality 72%

Adding clear duties, exact location, and timing helps reduce mismatched applications.

Checklist before publish
  • Use a clear role title
  • State exact pay and location
  • Explain shift timing and duration
  • Add realistic requirements
  • Set enough openings for the team size
Quick tip

For recurring event roles, one listing with the right openings count is cleaner than posting the same role multiple times.
